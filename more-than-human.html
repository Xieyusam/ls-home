While working on 'More Than Human,' I raised many questions. As the saying goes, 'You are not a fish; how do you know the happiness of the fish?' When we design for non-human beings, how can we know what their true needs are? Do mushroom spores really want to see things this way? Do plants really want to sing in these melodies? Perhaps what matters is that we are further building bridges between humans and non-humans, helping the broader public to understand more and continuously expand their perceptions—rather than seeking unchanging truths.
